Italian Sausage and White Beans

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon bottled minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 (19-ounce) can cannellini beans or other white beans, drained
  • 1 (14.5-ounce) can diced Italian-style tomatoes, undrained

Preparation

  1. Remove casings from sausage.
  2. Heat a large nonstick skillet coated with olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add bell pepper, onion, and garlic; sauté 3 minutes.
  4. Add sausage, and cook 8 minutes, or until browned, stirring to crumble.
  5. Stir in oregano and remaining ingredients; reduce heat to medium-low.
  6. Cook 5 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
